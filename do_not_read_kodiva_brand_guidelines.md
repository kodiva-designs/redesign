# **Kodiva Studio: Official Brand Guidelines**

### **1\. The Kodiva Core**

This is the DNA of our brand. It's the central idea that drives every decision, from design to communication.

* **Brand Essence:** The Smart, Scalable, & Human Tech Partner.  
* **Brand Promise:** We bridge your vision with future-ready technology, delivering professional-grade solutions that are both powerful and approachable.  
* **Brand Archetype:** A hybrid of **The Sage** (trusted, intelligent, expert) and **The Ally** (collaborative, friendly, supportive).

### **2\. Brand Personality & Voice**

This is how we sound. Our voice must be consistent across our website, proposals, social media, and client communication.

#### **Our Personality**

* **Innovative & Forward-Thinking:** We are experts in cutting-edge tech (AI, Web3) and speak about it with confidence and clarity.  
* **Professional & Sophisticated:** We are trusted partners. Our communication is clear, punctual, and purposeful. We build confidence through competence.  
* **Accessible & Collaborative:** We are "friendly experts." We break down complex ideas into simple, understandable terms. We are a partner, not just a vendor.  
* **Purposeful & Results-Driven:** We are not just about building *features*; we are about delivering *results* and helping our clients "level up."

#### **Voice & Tone**

| We Are... | We Are Not... |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Confident & Insightful** | Arrogant or Jargon-filled |
| **Clear & Direct** | Cold or Impersonal |
| **Collaborative & Supportive** | Overly Casual or "Salesy" |
| **Passionate & Innovative** | Hyped-up or Vague |

**Voice in Practice:**

* **Emphasis:** We use *italics* sparingly to emphasize key concepts, such as *value*, *our mission*, or *future-ready*.  
* **Example (Weak):** "We will utilize a Python-based AI microservice to optimize your data pipeline."  
* **Example (Strong):** "We'll build a smart AI integration that learns from your data, making your process *more efficient* and saving you valuable time."

### **3\. Logo & Visual Identity**

Our logo is the anchor of our high-tech, minimalist aesthetic. It should be treated with precision and respect.

#### **Logo**

* **Primary Logo:** The primary logo is the approved Kodiva Studio icon (minimalist, contemporary) paired with the wordmark.  
* **Wordmark:** "Kodiva Studio"  
* **Logomark (Icon):** The standalone minimalist icon.

#### **Logo Usage**

1. **Primary on Dark:** The all-white version of the logo is the default and should be used on all black or charcoal backgrounds.  
2. **Reverse on Light:** On the rare occasion a light background is used (e.g., a print document), the "Deep Onyx" version of the logo should be used.  
3. **Clear Space:** A margin of clear space, equal to the height of the "K" in "Kodiva," must be maintained around the logo at all times.  
4. **Don'ts:** Do not stretch, distort, re-color, or place the logo on a visually cluttered background.

### **4\. Color Palette**

Our palette is "dark-mode-first," built on a sophisticated monochromatic base with a single, high-impact digital accent. This creates a premium, focused, and high-tech feel.

#### **Primary Palette**

* **Deep Onyx (Primary Background)**  
  * HEX: \#0A0A0A  
  * RGB: 10, 10, 10  
  * *Use: All primary backgrounds. This near-black is softer than \#000 and feels more premium.*  
* **Pure White (Headlines)**  
  * HEX: \#FFFFFF  
  * *Use: All large, bold headlines and for the primary logo on dark backgrounds. Provides maximum contrast and impact.*

#### **Secondary & Text Palette**

* **Charcoal (UI Elements)**  
  * HEX: \#1E1E1E  
  * RGB: 30, 30, 30  
  * *Use: UI elements like cards, secondary backgrounds, and dividers. Creates a subtle, sophisticated layering effect on the Onyx background.*  
* **Smoke White (Body Text)**  
  * HEX: \#F5F5F5  
  * RGB: 245, 245, 245  
  * *Use: All body copy (paragraphs, subheadings). It is easier on the eyes for long-form reading than Pure White.*

#### **Accent Palette**

* **Cyber Teal (Accent)**  
  * HEX: \#00FFC2  
  * RGB: 0, 255, 194  
  * *Use: **Very sparingly.** This is our single, high-emphasis accent. Use for key call-to-action buttons, icons, or to highlight a single, critical piece of data. Its rarity gives it power.*

### **5\. Typography**

Our typography is clean, modern, and highly legible, reinforcing our minimalist aesthetic.

* **Primary Font Family: Inter**  
  * *Why?* Inter is a modern, highly-readable sans-serif that works perfectly for both sharp headlines and clean body text. It is professional and accessible. (Can be easily web-hosted via Google Fonts).

#### **Typographic Scale**

* **H1 \- Heading 1**  
  * Font: Inter Bold  
  * Size: 48px  
  * Color: Pure White (\#FFFFFF)  
  * *Use: Main page titles (e.g., "Delivering value across domains").*  
* **H2 \- Heading 2**  
  * Font: Inter SemiBold  
  * Size: 36px  
  * Color: Pure White (\#FFFFFF)  
  * *Use: Section titles.*  
* **H3 \- Heading 3**  
  * Font: Inter Bold  
  * Size: 24px  
  * Color: Smoke White (\#F5F5F5)  
  * *Use: Sub-section titles or large callouts.*  
* **Body Text**  
  * Font: Inter Regular  
  * Size: 16px (Line height: 1.5)  
  * Color: Smoke White (\#F5F5F5)  
  * *Use: All main paragraphs.*  
* **Emphasis**  
  * Font: Inter Italic or Inter Bold Italic  
  * *Use: To add emphasis to key concepts, as noted in the Voice section.*

### **6\. Visual System**

#### **UI Elements & Layout**

* **Negative Space:** Our design is clean and uncluttered. Generous use of "black" space (negative space) is critical to maintain a sophisticated, uncluttered feel.  
* **Card-Based UI:** Services and content blocks are organized into a card-based grid layout.  
* **Card Style:** Cards should use the Charcoal (\#1E1E1E) background, with generous internal padding and rounded corners (e.g., 8px-12px border-radius) to feel modern.  
* **Layouts:** Asymmetric layouts (e.g., two-column text and graphic) are encouraged to create dynamic, professional compositions.

#### **Iconography**

* **Style:** Icons must be simple, minimalist, and stylized.  
* **Type:** Outline-style (line art) icons are preferred. They must feel modern and light.  
* **Color:** Icons should be Smoke White (\#F5F5F5) or, for extreme emphasis, Cyber Teal (\#00FFC2).

#### **Photography**

* **Style:** Photography must be high-quality and match our premium aesthetic.  
* **Abstract & Tech:** Preferred style. Clean, beautiful, abstract 3D renders or data visualizations. These should be minimal and set against our dark backgrounds, often using hints of the Cyber Teal accent.  
* **Human & Collaborative:** When using photos of people, they must be high-contrast and professional. They should be in modern, well-lit environments that stand out against the dark UI. Avoid stiff, traditional corporate stock photos.
