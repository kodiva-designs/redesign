05 What clients say
06 Common questions

Answers before you have to ask.

How much does a redesign cost?

Every project is different, but most redesigns range from $4k to $20k. We'll give you a clear quote after your free audit call: no surprises, no hidden fees.

How long does a redesign take?

Most projects take 4 to 6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex builds like e-commerce or custom integrations may take 6 to 8 weeks. You'll get a timeline before we start.

Will I need to do anything?

We handle everything: design, development, migration, testing. You'll review at key milestones and give feedback, but the heavy lifting is on us.

Will I lose my Google rankings?

No. We implement proper 301 redirects, preserve your URL structure where it matters, and follow SEO best practices throughout. Most clients see rankings improve after launch.

What if I don't like the design?

You review and approve at every stage: wireframes, visual design, and development. We don't move forward until you're happy. Revisions are included.

Do you work with my industry?

We've redesigned sites for hospitality, e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, health & wellness, and more. If you have a business and need a website that works, we're in.
