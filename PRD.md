# Kodiva Studio Website Design Showcase Landing Page PRD

## 1. Product Summary

Build a landing page for **Kodiva Studio** that showcases premium website design work and makes visitors want to redesign their own website. The page should position Kodiva Studio as a smart, scalable, and human tech partner for property-led brands, especially hotels, villas, resorts, real estate developers, and premium service businesses.

The landing page must lead with visual proof. Visitors should quickly see latest Kodiva Studio design examples, understand why outdated websites reduce trust and conversion, then click into showcased designs or start a redesign conversation.

## Brand Context

Brand: **Kodiva Studio**

Brand Essence: `The Smart, Scalable, & Human Tech Partner`

Brand Promise: `We bridge your vision with future-ready technology, delivering professional-grade solutions that are both powerful and approachable.`

Brand Archetype: Sage + Ally — trusted, intelligent, expert, collaborative, friendly, and supportive.

Voice: confident, insightful, clear, direct, collaborative, and results-driven. Avoid arrogant, jargon-heavy, cold, overly casual, salesy, hyped-up, or vague language.

## 2. Primary Goal

Increase desire for website redesign by showing Kodiva Studio design examples and framing redesign as a business upgrade, not cosmetic work.

## 3. Primary CTA

**View Our Designs**

Purpose: drive visitors into the showcase section and live preview pages.

## 4. Secondary CTA

**Start Your Redesign**

Purpose: capture visitors who are ready to begin a redesign conversation.

## 5. Target Audience

### Primary Audience

- Hotel owners
- Villa operators
- Resort brands
- Real estate developers
- Property marketing teams
- Premium business owners with outdated websites

### Secondary Audience

- Agencies needing design references
- Founders preparing brand refresh
- Local businesses wanting a more premium digital presence

## 6. Audience Pain Points

- Current website feels outdated compared to property or brand quality.
- Website does not create trust within first few seconds.
- Mobile experience feels weak or slow.
- Design looks generic, template-based, or inconsistent.
- Visitors browse but do not inquire, book, or contact.
- Premium property feels less valuable online than in real life.
- Competitors look more polished and credible.

## 7. Core Positioning

### Positioning Statement

Kodiva Studio designs websites that make premium properties feel as desirable online as they do in person.

### Messaging Angle

A better website does not only look better. It changes how visitors judge your brand, trust your offer, and decide whether to book, inquire, or buy.

### Brand Promise

Kodiva Studio turns outdated websites into premium digital experiences that create trust, desire, and action.

## 8. Page Strategy

The page should follow this conversion path:

1. Show polished design immediately.
2. Make visitors compare it with their current website.
3. Explain why website redesign matters for trust and conversion.
4. Show latest design examples with clickable previews.
5. Explain design process and business outcomes.
6. Handle objections around cost, time, and complexity.
7. Invite visitors to view designs or start their redesign.

## 12. Functional Requirements

### Navigation

- Kodiva Studio logo / wordmark
- Work
- Industries
- Process
- FAQ
- CTA button: `View Our Designs`

### Showcase Grid

- Data-driven card list preferred.
- Each card fields:
  - `title`
  - `industry`
  - `description`
  - `image`
  - `url`
  - `featured`
- Cards redirect to linked design preview when clicked.
- Must support adding future designs without changing section structure.

### CTA Behavior

- `View Our Designs` scrolls to showcase section.
- Showcase card click opens target design URL.
- `Start Your Redesign` links to contact form, WhatsApp, booking page, or email. Final destination TBD.

### Responsive Requirements

- Desktop: 2-3 column showcase grid.
- Tablet: 2 column showcase grid.
- Mobile: 1 column showcase grid.
- CTAs stack on mobile.
- Hero preview collage must remain readable on small screens.

### Accessibility Requirements

- WCAG 2.2 AA target.
- High contrast for all text.
- Buttons and cards keyboard accessible.
- Showcase cards need descriptive alt text.
- Visible focus states.
- Avoid motion that blocks reading or clicking.
- Respect reduced-motion preference.
