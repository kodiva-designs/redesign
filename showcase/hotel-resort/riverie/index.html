A retreat for quiet days, open spaces, and natural rhythm.

Riverie brings together the architectural clarity of modernism and the serene luxury of Balinese slow living. Clean, horizontal planes meet texturized lime plaster, textured teak wood, coarse hand-woven linens, and basalt river stone.

The space is built to let the tropical environment breathe inside: morning shadows drifting over concrete floors, gentle cross-breezes flowing from palm treetops, and the calming presence of water at the center of the stay.

Location

Close enough to explore, quiet enough to retreat.

Tucked away from Bali's bustling coastal strips, Riverie lies nestled inside a peaceful jungle pocket, providing direct views of the natural landscape, yet remaining within easy access of cafes and local culture.

Neighborhood Seseh Valley, Canggu Outskirts, Bali
Airport Transit 45 minutes via private resort driver
Nearby Points Seseh Beach (8m), local temples, rice walking paths
