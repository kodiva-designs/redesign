Shaping Future Architecture

At Nilsson, we believe architecture is the most permanent form of cultural expression. Every structure we design is conceived not merely as a building, but as a contribution to the urban and natural landscape that will endure for generations.

Our practice is rooted in the Scandinavian tradition of material honesty and spatial clarity, informed by the rigorous discipline of Swiss modernism and the quiet confidence of Japanese spatial minimalism.

The Studio

Precision in Every Detail

Nilsson is an architecture and design studio operating at the intersection of Scandinavian minimalism and Swiss precision. Founded in Stockholm in 2008, we have grown to a team of 24 architects, designers, and visualizers working across Northern Europe.

Our process is collaborative and research-driven. We begin each project with an intensive study of context—geographic, cultural, and material—before a single line is drawn.

Case Study

Villa Skagen

Location Skagen, Denmark
Year 2023
Area 420 m²

Situated at the northernmost tip of Denmark where the North Sea meets the Baltic, Villa Skagen responds to one of Europe's most dramatic coastal landscapes with quiet confidence.

The residence is conceived as a series of interlocking concrete volumes that appear to emerge from the dunes themselves. Large-scale glazing frames views of the ever-changing sea, while the interior material palette—white oak, limestone, and polished concrete—creates a warm counterpoint to the harsh maritime climate.

The project represents our commitment to architecture that respects its context while pushing the boundaries of what is structurally and spatially possible.
