* Swiss International Style
* Scandinavian digital reduction
* Kinfolk-inspired travel editorial design
* Modern hospitality branding
* Apple-style interface restraint
* Airbnb experience storytelling
* Luxury eco-tourism publishing

# Borobudur Trip Design System

## Reconstructed Visual Language: Contemporary Heritage Editorialism

A high-fidelity editorial system inspired by:
* **The Boxed Page Canvas:** Centered desktop layout framing the entire site inside a floating capsule with soft outer drop shadows.
* **Rounded Card Modules:** Enclosing sections in structured containers with massive rounded corners (`24px`–`32px`).
* **Asymmetric Editorial Rhythm:** Juxtaposing wide landscape photography with clean, structured typographic blocks.
* **Signature Button System:** Double-pill interactions (text button paired with a separate circular arrow button).
* **Quiet Luxury Branding:** Understated color palette derived from Javanese geology, morning mist, and golden hour light.

---

## 1. Canvas & Layout Structure

### Page Wrapper (The Floating Card)
* **Behavior:** Centered viewport container on desktop, mimicking a premium magazine layout.
* **Page Background:** Soft Warm Gray (`#F3F2F0` / `#F5F4F2`).
* **Canvas Background:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`).
* **Border Radius:** `40px` on desktop (collapses to `0` on mobile).
* **Outer Margin:** `40px` top/bottom, auto left/right.
* **Shadow:** `0px 40px 120px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.06), 0px 10px 40px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.02)`.
* **Width:** Max-width of `1400px`.

### Section Spacing
* **Rhythm:** `100px` to `140px` vertical padding between sections inside the canvas.
* **Margins:** All main card elements (Hero, CTA, Footer) are nested within the page wrapper with a uniform margin of `24px` to `32px`.

## 3. Typography Hierarchy

### Headings (Neo-Grotesque Sans)
* **Font Family:** `Inter`, Sans-serif.
* **Weights:** `300` (Light) and `400` (Regular).
* **Letter Spacing:** `-1.5%` to `-2.5%`.
* **Italic Use:** Regular fonts styled with `em` tags (`font-style: italic`) for editorial accents.

### Typography Scale
* **Hero Title:** `clamp(36px, 5.5vw, 68px)`
* **Section Heading (H2):** `clamp(28px, 3.2vw, 44px)`
* **Sub-Heading (H3):** `clamp(20px, 1.8vw, 28px)`
* **Body Text:** `16px` – `17px` (`line-height: 1.65`, Volcanic Slate color)
* **Metadata/Labels:** `11px` (uppercase, letter-spacing `0.2em`)

---

## 4. Key Interface Signatures

### Inside-Hero Navigation
* **Placement:** Positioned at the top of the canvas, outside the hero card, with a clean border divider.
* **Elements:**
  - Left: Minimalist logo containing a simplified Stupa vector mark + "Borobudur Trip".
  - Center: Sub-label "Based in: Central Java, Indonesia" to anchor geographical authenticity.
  - Right: Editorial navigation links and a thin-outline planning button.

### Asymmetrical Hero Content Layout
* **Hero Card:** Nestled in the canvas with `border-radius: 32px` and `24px` margin.
* **Header:** Left-aligned, bold, multi-line statement.
* **Description:** Placed at the bottom-right, balancing the header.
* **Stats Pill:** Bottom-left pill indicating a crucial statistic (e.g. `2,672 Relief Panels`) next to a dark translucent indicator pill with 4 small icons (Stupa, Sun, Mist, Mountain).
* **Badge Row:** Bottom-right horizontal container with three dark semi-transparent pill badges:
  - `UNESCO Heritage Site`
  - `Scholar-Guided`
  - `Dawn Entry`

### Double-Pill Button System
* **Structure:** A long pill-shaped button with text, followed by a separate, circular button containing a diagonal arrow icon (`↗`).
* **Interaction:** On hover, the text button slightly scales or darkens, and the arrow button rotates `45deg`.

### Rounded Card Grids
* **Experiences:** A 4-column layout displaying cards with large rounded corners (`20px`).
* **Explore Grid:** Asymmetric grid containing 1 large vertical card and 1 smaller horizontal card on the right, balanced by descriptive typography on the left.

---

## 5. Implementation Rules

1. **No full-bleed sections except inside card boundaries:** The page itself must feel bounded by the floating page canvas.
2. **Strict negative space:** Maintain a 30% text to 70% whitespace/imagery ratio in editorial spreads.
3. **No generic arrows:** Use the circular diagonal arrow (`↗`) for secondary transitions and standard buttons.
4. **Rich transitions:** Maintain a smooth scroll (Lenis) and organic GSAP scroll reveals (fade-in, parallax).
